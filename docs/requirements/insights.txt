PAGE PURPOSE
The Insights page is the public doctrine layer of Axis.
This page should not feel like a blog, news feed, or content hub.
It should feel like a private strategic archive where Axis publishes frameworks, operating principles, and high-level thinking for organizations building toward structured scale.
The visitor should leave this page thinking:
Axis does not just execute. Axis thinks at the system level.
